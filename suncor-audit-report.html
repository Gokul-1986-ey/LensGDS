Section 02
Target Company Overview
Suncor Energy Inc. — Canada's leading integrated energy company

Suncor Energy is a globally competitive integrated energy company headquartered in Calgary, Alberta. Unlike pure upstream producers, Suncor's vertical integration — from oil sands extraction through upgrading, refining, and retail distribution — creates a unique and defensible supply chain model. Its ownership of the Petro-Canada network (1,800+ locations) and Canada's Electric Highway positions it as the only Canadian energy company with a coast-to-coast consumer fuel and EV charging presence.

Section 03
Industry Overview & Value Chain
Canadian oil sands industry structure, key trends, and end-to-end supply chain mapping

The Canadian energy sector is dominated by oil sands production, which accounts for the vast majority of Canada's ~5M boe/d output. The top five operators — Suncor, Canadian Natural Resources (CNR), Cenovus, Imperial Oil, and ConocoPhillips — control approximately 85% of Alberta's oil sands production. In 2026, combined output from the big four (Suncor, CNR, Cenovus, Imperial) is projected at 3.9M boe/d, with 75% concentrated in Alberta's oil sands.

Section 09
Strategic Recommendations
Top 10 prioritized initiatives — ranked by impact vs. effort, for CSCO / COO / CXO audience

Quick Wins (0–30 Days)

Rec #1
SKU Rationalization & Excess Inventory Reduction
Audit slow-moving & obsolete refined product SKUs across 4 refineries. Target 10% DIO reduction (from ~18 to ~16 days) to free ~$400M in working capital.
High Impact
Rec #2
DSO Reduction via AR Automation
Implement e-invoicing and early payment incentives for Petro-Canada wholesale customers. Target DSO from ~35 to ~28 days, releasing ~$1.0B in receivables.
High Impact
Rec #3
Renegotiate Key Supplier Payment Terms
Extend DPO from ~45 to ~52 days for top 500 strategic vendors. At Suncor's spend scale, every 5 days DPO = ~$500M+ in free float.
High Impact

Mid-Term (3–6 Months)

Rec #4
AI-Powered Demand Forecasting & S&OP Transformation
Deploy ML-based demand sensing across refining and retail segments. Target forecast accuracy improvement from ~75% to ~88%, reducing inventory buffer stock by 15%.
High Impact
Rec #5
Vendor Base Consolidation Program
Rationalize from 5,212 to ~3,500 strategic vendors via category management. Target 5-8% procurement cost reduction (~$200-300M at Suncor's spend scale).
Medium Impact
Rec #6
Upgrader Reliability Improvement Program
Build on coke drum replacement success at Base Plant. Reduce unplanned downtime by 20% to protect per-barrel operating cost advantage vs. CNR's $40 breakeven.
High Impact

Strategic Bets (6–18 Months)

Rec #7
Deploy Integrated Supply Chain Control Tower
Build end-to-end supply chain visibility from well-head to pump. Connects upstream production, refinery throughput, logistics, and Petro-Canada retail in a single data layer. Closes 2-point digital gap vs. Imperial.
High Impact
Rec #8
Scale SAF & Low-Carbon Fuels Production
Accelerate Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) production at Montreal Refinery and pursue additional low-carbon refining conversions. Positions Suncor uniquely vs. all three competitors.
High Impact
Rec #9
Dual-Source Critical Upstream Suppliers
Implement dual-sourcing for top 50 critical maintenance & reagent suppliers to reduce concentration risk. Target: no single vendor representing >5% of a category spend.
Medium Impact
Rec #10
Expand Canada's Electric Highway as Revenue Stream
Monetize EV charging infrastructure via B2B fleet partnerships and subscription models. This transforms a CapEx investment into a recurring revenue stream, hedging against gasoline demand erosion.
High Impact
